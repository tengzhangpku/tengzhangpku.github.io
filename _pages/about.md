Biography
======
Zhang Teng is a research assistant professor at the School of Computer Science, Peking University. He has long been engaged in research on post-Moore devices and integration technology, in-memory-computing, in-sensor-computing, hardware-software co-optimization. He has published over 60 papers in top-tier journals and conferences (with 10+ as the first or corresponding author) in Nature Electronics, Nature Communications, Advanced Materials, National Science Review, IEDM/JSSC/ESSERC, among others. He has been cited more than 3,800 times, with an H-index of 26. Several of his papers have been recognized as ESI Highly Cited Papers, featured as journal covers, or selected as Editors' Picks. He is also the primary inventor for over ten patents in China and the U.S. He serves as a reviewer for journals and conferences such as Nature Communications, IEEE TED, IEEE EDL, Communications Engineering, and Frontiers in Neuroscience.
